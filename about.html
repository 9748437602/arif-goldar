About Arif Goldar

Building dreams, creating value, and transforming the real estate landscape of Kolkata since 2010

Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming one of Kolkata's most trusted real estate partners

2010

Foundation

Arif Goldar started with a vision to provide quality housing solutions to the people of Kolkata, beginning with our first residential project in Chandpur.

2014

Expansion

Expanded our services to include commercial real estate and property management, establishing ourselves as a full-service real estate company.

2018

Innovation

Introduced innovative financial services and partnerships to help clients achieve their dream of home ownership through flexible payment options.

2023

Transformation

Launched our digital platform, integrating technology with real estate to provide seamless customer experiences and expanded our footprint across West Bengal.
