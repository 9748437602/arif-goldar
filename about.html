Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Arif Goldar, our company began with a simple vision: to provide quality housing solutions that combine modern amenities with affordability. What started as a small real estate venture has now grown into one of Kolkata's most trusted names in property development.

Over the years, we've completed numerous residential and commercial projects, each reflecting our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Our journey has been marked by milestones of growth, learning, and continuous improvement.

Today, we stand as a testament to what vision, dedication, and hard work can achieve. But our story is far from over - we're constantly evolving, exploring new possibilities, and striving to set new benchmarks in the real estate industry.
